Channel Themed Examples
Branding is very important to Twitch creators. Many creators upload their own branded header images for the panels on their About Page. Consider allowing creators to specify their brand colors and even Google Fonts to create a UI that matches the brand.
The examples below use the dark mode of the default purple theme as the base, then overwrite some of the colors and fonts with streamer's brand colors, accent color, and Google Fonts defined. The overwriting CSS can be found within the <style> element, inside the <head> section in these HTML files.
